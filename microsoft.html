Meetings are just one tool in our tool belt Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is a business communication platform deisgned to help business workers stay connected and productive. Teams integrates chat, video conferencing, file sharing tools into a single interface, reducing the need for multiple apps. This integration extends to Microsoft 365 services like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, giving users flexible and convenient access to shared files within the Teams environment.
